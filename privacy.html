Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Digital Operating LLC ("AI Digital Labs," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our AI-powered marketing tools via our websites, APIs, MCP (Model Context Protocol) integrations, and embedded widgets (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and organization affiliation via our authentication provider (Clerk). We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Clerk.

Usage Data. We collect information about how you use the Services, including:

  • Which tools you use and how often (e.g., number of website audits, scans)
  • Timestamps of requests and session durations
  • API request metadata (endpoint, status code, response time)

Content You Submit. When you use our tools, you provide input data such as:

  • Website URLs for auditing
  • Creative asset URLs for neuromarketing analysis
  • Prompts for engineering and optimization
  • Brand names, product descriptions, and business context

MCP Integration Data. When you connect via Claude or other MCP-compatible assistants, we collect:

  • OAuth authentication tokens (stored as cryptographic hashes only)
  • Tool invocation requests and parameters
  • We do not receive or store your conversations with Claude — only the specific tool calls and their parameters

4. Data Retention

  • Audit reports and session data: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after account closure.
  • API request logs: Retained for 90 days, then automatically purged.
  • OAuth tokens: Access tokens expire after 1 hour. Refresh tokens expire after 90 days. Revoked tokens are retained (as hashes only) for 30 days for security audit purposes.
  • Account information: Retained until you request deletion or your organization's subscription ends plus 90 days.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • API keys and OAuth tokens stored as SHA-256 hashes — we never store plaintext credentials
  • Row-level security (RLS) in our database ensures users can only access their own data
  • Authentication via Clerk with secure session management
  • Infrastructure hosted in AWS US-East-1 (Supabase) and Netlify's global CDN

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@aidigital.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our Services use essential cookies for authentication (Clerk session cookies). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies. We do not track you across third-party websites.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Our Services are hosted in the United States. If you access the Services from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. We rely on standard contractual clauses and our service providers' compliance programs for lawful international transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance.
